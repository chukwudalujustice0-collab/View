Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps so your WhatsApp connection works properly.

1

Open Meta Developers

Create or open your app and add the WhatsApp product.

2

Get your IDs

Copy the WhatsApp Business Account ID and Phone Number ID.

3

Create your verify token

Use the same verify token here and in Meta webhook verification.

4

Paste webhook callback URL

Use the generated callback URL on this page inside your Meta webhook settings.

5

Save and test

After saving, use the test buttons to confirm your token can access the number details.
