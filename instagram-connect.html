Before You Connect

📸

Use a professional Instagram account

For publishing APIs, Instagram usually expects a Business or Creator account.

📘

Link Instagram to a Facebook Page

This is commonly required for Meta permissions used in publishing and account access.

🛡️

Complete app permissions on your backend

Your backend auth route should request the right Meta permissions before redirecting back.

🎯

Set your callback correctly

Your backend should return to this page after authentication with success or error details.

How This Page Works

1

Tap Connect Instagram

The page opens your backend auth route for Instagram connection.

2

Complete Instagram and Meta login

After approval, your backend sends the user back here with the connection result.

3

View stores the connected account locally

This page saves the Instagram connection on the device so your connected accounts UI can read it.

4

Use it in your publish flow later

Your posting pages can read the saved account and send publish requests through your backend.

Backend Route Setup

This page expects your server to start Instagram OAuth from a route like:

Suggested auth route
/api/instagram/auth/start

After success, return to this page with values like:

Example callback query
status=success, provider=instagram, username, account_name, account_id

Access tokens should ideally be stored securely on your backend, not permanently in the browser.
