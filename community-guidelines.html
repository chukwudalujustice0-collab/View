Safety • Respect • Integrity

Help us keep View safe, respectful, and useful for everyone.

These Community Guidelines explain what is allowed and what is not allowed on View. They apply to posts, comments, captions, images, videos, chats, group messages, profile names, usernames, bios, live features, connected account activity, and any content or behavior on the platform.

Effective date: March 30, 2026 Applies to: Posts, chats, groups, media, profiles Contact: info.ceeticegroup@gmail.com

Quick summary

Be respectful No bullying, harassment, impersonation, or hateful behavior.
Be honest No scams, fraud, fake giveaways, deceptive links, or misinformation meant to mislead.
Be responsible Only share content you own or have permission to use.
Be safe No dangerous threats, criminal promotion, or content that puts people at risk.

1. Our Goal

View is built for connection, creativity, communication, and community. We want people to share ideas, post content, chat with others, and build meaningful interactions in a safe environment.

Content or behavior that damages trust, encourages abuse, or puts people at risk may be removed, restricted, or reported where required.

2. Respectful Behavior

Use View in a way that respects other people.

Allowed

Debate, disagreement, criticism, humor, and opinion shared without abuse or targeted harm.

Not allowed

Insults meant to humiliate, repeated targeting, intimidation, humiliation campaigns, or degrading treatment.

Strong opinions are allowed, but targeted cruelty, repeated abuse, and deliberate attempts to make someone unsafe are not.

3. Harassment & Bullying

Do not harass, bully, or stalk others on View.

  • No repeated abusive messages, comments, tags, or mentions.
  • No threats, intimidation, blackmail, or attempts to silence people through fear.
  • No coordinated attacks, pile-ons, or encouraging others to target someone.
  • No mocking someone’s private tragedy, disability, appearance, or vulnerability to shame them.
Accounts that repeatedly target other users may face content removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent suspension.

4. Hate, Extremism & Degrading Content

We do not allow content that glorifies hate, dehumanizes people, or promotes extremist violence or organized hatred.

  • No content praising or supporting violent extremist groups.
  • No slurs or degrading language used to attack protected groups.
  • No content encouraging exclusion, harm, or violence against people because of who they are.
  • No organized hate symbols or propaganda shared for support or recruitment.

Context matters. Educational, documentary, reporting, or anti-hate discussion may be allowed when clearly presented for awareness or criticism, not promotion.

5. Safety & Violent Threats

Do not post or send content that threatens serious harm or encourages dangerous acts.

  • No credible threats of violence against any person, group, or place.
  • No instructions or recruitment for violent wrongdoing or criminal harm.
  • No glorification of attacks, killings, kidnappings, or organized violence.
  • No content celebrating harm to real people.
We may act quickly on urgent safety threats, including removing content, restricting accounts, and responding to lawful safety requests.

6. Fraud, Scams & Deception

View must not be used to scam, deceive, or manipulate people for money, access, traffic, or personal gain.

  • No fake giveaways, fake investments, fake jobs, or fake urgent requests.
  • No phishing links, account-stealing messages, or fake support accounts.
  • No impersonation for financial gain, manipulation, or reputation damage.
  • No misleading claims designed to trick users into sending money, data, or credentials.
Accounts involved in fraud or impersonation may be disabled immediately.

7. Sexual Content & Exploitation

Do not use View to exploit, coerce, expose, or endanger people through sexual content.

  • No sexual exploitation, coercion, blackmail, or non-consensual sharing.
  • No sexualized content involving minors, or any exploitative content involving young people.
  • No grooming, predatory behavior, or attempts to obtain exploitative private material.
  • No sharing of intimate images or videos without consent.
Serious violations may lead to permanent removal and additional action where legally required.

8. Privacy & Personal Information

Respect people’s privacy. Do not expose private or sensitive information without permission.

  • No sharing of private phone numbers, addresses, identity numbers, bank details, passwords, or access codes without consent.
  • No doxxing, location exposure, or targeted leaking of personal information.
  • No posting private conversations, recordings, or images in a harmful or deceptive way where you do not have the right to share them.
  • No attempts to trick users into revealing personal or financial information.

9. Copyright, Identity & Authenticity

Share original work, properly licensed content, or content you have the right to post.

  • No repeated copyright infringement or unauthorized reposting.
  • No pretending to be another person, brand, creator, organization, or public figure in a misleading way.
  • No fake verification, fake identity badges, or edited context intended to mislead others.
  • Parody, commentary, or fan expression may be allowed when it is clearly not deceptive.

10. Spam, Manipulation & Platform Abuse

Do not abuse View’s systems, reach, or engagement tools.

  • No mass unsolicited messages, repetitive comments, or fake engagement loops.
  • No bots or scripts used to manipulate likes, follows, views, trends, or rankings without permission.
  • No link flooding, repetitive posting, or misleading clickbait designed only to exploit traffic.
  • No attempts to bypass restrictions, evade bans, or recreate abusive accounts repeatedly.
Healthy growth

Build genuine communities, real conversations, and original content.

Unhealthy manipulation

Fake followers, spam messages, bot abuse, and forced engagement schemes.

11. Groups, Chats & Community Spaces

These rules also apply inside private and public spaces on View, including group chats, comment sections, live rooms, creator communities, channels, and collaborative spaces.

  • Group admins should help keep their spaces lawful, respectful, and safe.
  • Private groups are not exempt from abuse, fraud, exploitation, or harmful coordination rules.
  • Repeated harmful behavior inside closed spaces may still result in moderation action.
Privacy settings do not override platform safety rules.

12. Enforcement

When content or behavior breaks these guidelines, we may take action depending on severity, pattern, and risk.

  • Remove content
  • Limit visibility or distribution
  • Disable messaging or posting features
  • Issue warnings
  • Temporarily suspend accounts
  • Permanently remove accounts
  • Restrict connected features or linked accounts

Severe or repeated abuse usually leads to stronger enforcement.

13. Reporting & Appeals

If you see content or behavior that violates these guidelines, report it. If your account or content is affected by moderation and a review option is available, you may submit an appeal through the appropriate channel.

  • Report harassment, impersonation, scams, spam, or harmful content.
  • Include clear details, screenshots, usernames, or links where possible.
  • False reporting or abuse of reporting tools may also lead to action.
The strongest communities are built when users help protect each other.

Need to report abuse or ask about a moderation action?

Contact support for reporting, account review questions, safety concerns, or community policy issues.
