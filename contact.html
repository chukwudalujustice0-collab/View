Frequently asked questions

Common support topics for users, creators, and partners.

I cannot log in to my account. What should I do?

Use the forgot password option on the login page first. If the issue continues, contact support and include your username and account email.

How do I report a fake account or scam?

Send a safety report with the username, profile link, screenshots, and a short explanation of what happened.

Can I request removal of content that affects me?

Yes. Contact support with the exact content link, account details, and why the content should be reviewed.

How do I ask about partnerships or integration?

Choose Business Enquiry in the form and provide your brand, goal, and the type of partnership you want to discuss.

How fast will support respond?

Response time may vary depending on message volume and the type of issue, especially for safety or legal matters.

Prefer direct contact?

You can email us directly for support, moderation, reports, or business communication regarding the View platform.
