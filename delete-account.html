What happens when you delete your account

The exact handling may depend on the type of data, technical setup, and any legal or safety obligations.

Profile and access

Your profile and account access may be disabled, removed, or scheduled for deletion.

Posts and uploaded content

Your public content may be removed or disassociated, depending on how the platform stores and displays it.

Messages and interactions

Some message history or conversation records may remain visible to other participants where technically necessary.

Backups and logs

Backups, diagnostics, and security logs may remain for a limited period before expiry or removal.

Legal and fraud-related retention

Some records may be retained where required to comply with law, enforce policies, investigate abuse, or prevent fraud.

If your issue is temporary, you may prefer changing notification settings, updating your privacy options, or contacting support before deleting your account.

Need help before deleting your account?

If you are facing a login issue, privacy concern, harassment issue, or app problem, contact support first. Some issues can be resolved without losing your account.
