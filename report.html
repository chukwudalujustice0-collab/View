Tips for a stronger report

Clear and complete reports are easier to review.

Include usernames or links

If you know the exact account, profile, or post involved, include it.

State what happened clearly

Explain the issue in simple words, including dates or repeated behavior if relevant.

Describe the harm or concern

Say whether the issue involves abuse, fraud, impersonation, privacy, or a broken feature.

Use one report per issue when possible

Separate unrelated issues so they can be handled more accurately.

For technical issues

Include device type, browser, page name, and what happened before the issue appeared.

You can also contact the View team directly for moderation concerns, account problems, legal notices, or other platform issues.
